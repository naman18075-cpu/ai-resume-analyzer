How it works

A simple two-sided platform structure that is easy to pitch.

01

Client posts the job

The client enters project details, budget, skills needed, and deadline.

02

Freelancers place bids

Interested freelancers submit pricing, delivery timeline, and a short proposal.

03

Verification builds trust

The platform collects PAN, name, age, contact details, and profile data before approval.

04

Client compares and hires

The client reviews bids, ratings, verification status, and selects the best freelancer.
